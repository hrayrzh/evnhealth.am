ABOUT

Environment and Health NGO (ENVI&Health) is established in October 2011 by young enthusiasts, representing various CSOs, state bodies, Academy, as well as individuals, who are concerned about environmental and health care issues. The NGO located in Armenia and act globally. ENVI&Health aims to promote an establishment clean and healthy environment through the youth sector, encouraging youth to take responsibility for the environment conservation and decision-making. Emphasizing the potential of youth in general, ENVI&Health highly welcomes the new creative initiatives and is eager to generate innovative ideas for the solutions to environmental and health care issues.

What we do

ENVI&Health focuses on developing effective and sustainable preventive policies, innovations, and interventions to enhance environmental conservation and public health. We act through youth to foster public awareness-raising, environmental education and the introduction of innovative technologies and ideas that promote green solutions. We believe that together we can keep the Earth green and sustainable for future generations.

Our Vision & Mission

ENVI&Health VISION is together we can make Earth Green for our future. Our MISION is to create a platform for the exchange of knowledge, ideas and expertise among young people from all over the world, to foster youth involvement in nature conservation and decision-making processes based on a better scientific understanding of the environmental issues and determinant of public health.

PROJECTS

Eco-learning through Gaming

Duration: Launched in January 2020, continuing

Funding organization: Self-financing initiative

The aims of the initiative are to raise public awareness of environmental issues among schoolchildren through games. The objectives of the initiative are to develop and organize special educational interactive games and discussions for children to spend an interesting time and get to know the problems of the environment. For this, the EcoShutHasik series of games have been developed, addressing the issues of waste, climate change, water, biomaterials cycles, etc.

Series of thematic seminars/webinars on environmental issues

Duration: Launched in January 2020, continuing

Funding organization: Self-financing initiative

The aims of the initiative are to raise public awareness of environmental issues, foster collaboration between specialists of relevant fields and academy, and contribute to educating an environmentally conscious generation.

Youth for a clean planet

Duration: Aug-Dec 2020

Funding organization: RA Ministry of Education, Science, Culture and Sports

The main goal of the project is to develop eco-education among the youth and to promote the role of youth in solving environmental problems. The objectives are to unite young educators, students and schoolchildren on environmental issues at the local level, to present non-formal educational eco-games, to strengthen the "school-university" cooperation platform.

Community without waste

Duration: Jan-Dec 2020

Funding organization: ACBA Federation and Yerevan Impact Hub

The aim of this startup is to develop ways of collecting and recycling waste in a rural community through the use of state-of-the-art technologies and creative ideas.

Youth capacity building for the rural community development and implementation of the SDGs

Duration: May-Oct 2020

Funding organization: Embassy of Czech Republic in Yerevan

The overall objective of the project is to contribute to local community development the local level by constructing a public eco-playground and raise public awareness for improving the environmental understanding, knowledge and public health for taking actions to achieve the SDGs under 2030 Agenda in Armenia.

Promoting eco-education among youth through non-formal learning mechanisms

Duration: December 2019

Funding organization: RA RA Ministry of Education, Science, Culture and Sports

The main goal of the project is to unite young educators at the national level, on environmental issues and create a "school-academy" bilateral cooperation platform. The project objectives are to promote the development of eco-education in the schools in the marzes of Armenia and foster the involvement of youth in environmental activities, as well as to encourage public interest in the profession of "ecology" and contribute to the learning of future young environmentalists.
